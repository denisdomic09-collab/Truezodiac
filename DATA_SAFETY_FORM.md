## Data Collection
The TrueZodiac app may collect the following types of data:
- **Personal Data**: Information provided by users such as name, email address, and phone number.
- **Usage Data**: Information about how the app is accessed and used.
- **Device Information**: Data regarding the device used to access the app, including device type, operating system, and app version.

## Security
We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Measures include:
- Encryption of sensitive information transmitted online.
- Regular security assessments and updates to our database.
- Restricted access to personal information to authorized personnel only.

## Third-party Sharing
We do not sell or trade users' personal identification information to third parties. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our business partners for the following purposes:
- To help them understand our users and improve their services.

## Data Practices
- We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected.
- Users are encouraged to review their personal information regularly and request any changes or deletions as necessary.

## Compliance Standards
The TrueZodiac app is committed to complying with all relevant data protection regulations, including:
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

## User Rights
Users have the right to:
- Access their personal information.
- Request correction of inaccurate personal information.
- Request deletion of their personal information.

## Breach Notification
In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach and follow all necessary legal requirements.
