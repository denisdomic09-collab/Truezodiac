# APP PRIVACY MANIFEST FOR TRUEZODIAC APP

## Data Types Collected
- **Personal Information**: Name, email address, phone number, and payment information.
- **Usage Data**: Information on how users interact with the app, including access times and features used.
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

## Data Use Purposes
- To provide and maintain our service
- To improve user experience
- To process transactions and send confirmations
- To communicate with users regarding updates and offers

## Tracking Practices
- We utilize analytics tools to track usage metrics and app performance. 
- Tracking technologies include cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience.

## Data Security Measures
- All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. 
- We implement industry-standard security measures to safeguard personal information. 

## Third-Party Services
- We may use third-party services for analytics, marketing, and payment processing, such as:
  - Google Analytics
  - Stripe for payment processing

## User Privacy Controls
- Users can access and manage their data through the app settings.
- Users can opt out of tracking and data collection at any time.
