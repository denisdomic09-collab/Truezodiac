## 2. The architecture, in plain words

TrueZodiac runs entirely on your device. There is no backend server controlled by the Developer. There are no cloud accounts. There is no login or registration.

Concretely, this means:

- Your astrological chart is computed locally using astronomical algorithms (Meeus) embedded in the App and validated against IAU sources
- Your horoscope text is generated locally on your device from a deterministic phrase pool stored in the application bundle
- Your personal information remains in iOS's local storage (UserDefaults and the application sandbox)
- Nothing is uploaded, synchronized, or backed up to any server we operate

---

## 3. Information processed locally on your device

When you use the App, the following information is processed **on your iPhone or iPad only**:

**Astrological inputs:**
- Date of birth, time of birth, place of birth (city / coordinates)
- Computed astronomical positions (Sun, Moon, Ascendant, Descendant) under the IAU 13-constellation real zodiac system, including Ophiuchus

**Personalization (each field is optional and may be skipped):**
- Name or chosen alias
- Gender
- Relationship status
- Profession
- Parenting status
- Current focus area (e.g. work, relationships, health, spiritual growth)

**Application state:**
- Language preference, notification settings, theme preferences

This information is stored exclusively in the App's iOS sandbox, protected by iOS's standard encryption when your device passcode is enabled.

## 5. iCloud Backup (under your control)

If you have iCloud Backup enabled in your iPhone Settings, iOS may include the App's local data in your encrypted iCloud backup. This backup is governed exclusively by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) and the encryption keys are controlled by you and Apple, **not** by the Developer. The Developer has no access to iCloud backups.

You can disable iCloud Backup for TrueZodiac in **Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → iCloud Backup → Apps**.

## 7. Push notifications

If you grant the App permission to send notifications (for example, daily horoscope reminders), notification delivery uses Apple's Push Notification Service (APNs). The **content of every notification is generated locally on your device** — the Developer does not transmit personalized content through APNs.

You can revoke notification permission at any time via **Settings → Notifications → TrueZodiac**.

## 8. iOS-level diagnostics (Apple-controlled)

iOS may collect anonymous diagnostic data at the operating-system level if you have enabled **"Share with App Developers"** in **Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements**. When provided, this data is aggregated and anonymized by Apple before reaching the Developer, contains no personal identifiers, and is governed by Apple's policies — not ours.

You can disable this at any time in your iPhone Settings.
